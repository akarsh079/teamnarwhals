About Me

I'm the vice president of Skywalkers and the club treasurer of AHS VEX/ Beyond music and robotics, I'm also an active member of Scouts BSA. Scouting has instilled in me values of leadership, character, and community service. I'm currently working on my Eagle Project, a key milestone in my scouting journey.
